Journal for Week of March 5 to 11, 2017

Day 1 - Sunday, March 5: The Plan

This week I plan direct students into group projects that will allow them to work collaboratively. There are several projects that have emerged either directly from student interests or from our Career Center community. Two in particular are a senior project idea from one student to create a mobile app for Wakefield High School students and a website for the interdisciplinary Monarch butterfly project at the Arlington Career Center. The goal will be to get most students hooked up with a project over the next few weeks.

Personal Learning Goals

In terms of my own self-directed learning, I plan to investigate whether the soon to be rolled out App Lab export feature that Paul Carduner told me is ready for testing would be a viable solution for the Wakefield High School app.

Time permitting I will also continue studying Flask, the the specific goal of building on the microblog I created from the official website Tutorial last week. I'll choose some combination of the following three tasks to focus on:

  1. Add proper authentication
  2. Add support for writing blog posts in ReStructuredText.
  3. Improve the appearance of the current templates.
